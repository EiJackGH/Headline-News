Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

At Headline News, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Our platform is designed to prioritize user privacy and limit data collection. Here is what we do and do not collect:

  • Client-Side Storage: We do not store your reading preferences, bookmarks, simulator balances, or customized visual themes on our servers. All such data is saved directly on your device via client-side LocalStorage and can be cleared by you at any time.
  • Server Logs: When you access our website, our servers may log standard connection information such as IP address, browser type, and access times to ensure platform stability and security.
  • AI Extraction Requests: When you paste a URL into our AI Article Extractor, the URL is processed on-the-fly to scrape and summarize text. We do not store or index these custom extracted articles or link them to your personal identity.

2. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or sell your personal browsing habits to third-party advertisers. All theme choices and personalization are executed entirely client-side without permanent tracking cookies.

3. Third-Party Services & Links

Our Service contains links to external news sites, social networks, and CDNs (such as Tailwind CSS, FontAwesome, and Chart.js). These third-party websites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or data collection practices.

4. Data Security

We employ standard, industry-accepted security measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of raw platform traffic. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us through our Contact page.
