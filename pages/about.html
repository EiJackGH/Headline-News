About Us

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Headline News is a cutting-edge, state-of-the-art news aggregation and automated intelligence extraction platform designed for modern, tech-savvy readers.

Our Mission

In an era of information overload and noisy feeds, our mission is to deliver high-quality, readable, and highly focused coverage across five essential sectors: Technology, Finance, Space & Science, Biotech, and Entertainment. We combine classic, robust curation with advanced client-side interactive modules to make tracking the world simple and distraction-free.

Key Features

AI Article Extractor

Paste any URL to instantly scrape clean text, bypass heavy popups, and analyze sentiment, named entities, and key bulleted takeaways.

Interactive Market Watch

Get high-fidelity sparklines and simulated historical asset tracking. Run simulated buy and sell orders on paper trading balances.

Premium Accessible Design

Read comfortably under any light with four fully optimized color schemas—Light, Dark, Sepia, and High Contrast. Customize font scaling, sans/serif styling, and line height adjustments.

Real-time AI Text-to-Speech

Listen to standard articles on-the-go with fully integrated, customizable voice selection and speed-controlled Web Speech API narration.

Why We Are Different

Unlike other modern news outlets, we do not require accounts, passwords, or cloud syncing. Your custom setup remains entirely your own, saved locally on your browser. Zero tracking, zero third-party profiling, and complete respect for readability and user comfort.
