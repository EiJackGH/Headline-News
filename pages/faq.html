Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform, real-time news indexing, and the AI Scraper Engine below.

Q. How does the AI Article Extractor work?

When you provide a URL, our backend connects to the site, isolates the readable main article content (discarding intrusive ads, dynamic script blocks, cookie walls, and side widgets), runs named entity detection, assesses reading metrics, and synthesizes key takeaways using NLP pipelines.

Q. Is my simulation trading data and cash saved?

Yes! All simulation balances, cash amounts, and owned shares are saved locally directly inside your browser's LocalStorage. Clearing your browser cookies or storage will reset your values to the default $10,000 cash balance.

Q. How do I adjust the reading layout?

Use the Reader Experience panel in the sidebar (right column on desktop) to scale text sizes from SM to XL, adjust fonts (Sans, Serif, Mono), and switch line height spacing (Tight, Normal, Loose) to fit your optimal comfort layout.

Q. Why did an extraction request fail?

Some sites block automated user agents behind rigorous Cloudflare or paywall challenges, which can prevent clean readability markup scraping. We continually update our scraping bypass routines. For testing, please use our test sample buttons.
